## Overview

This document explains what each type of user can do in the MagneQ system. It shows the different features and capabilities available to administrators, sales staff, production workers, and customers.

---

## User Roles Summary

| Role | Description | What They Do | What They Can Access |
|------|-------------|--------------|---------------------|
| **ADMIN** | System Administrator | Manages everything | All features and data |
| **DEVELOPER** | System Developer | Sets up products and users | Product catalog and user management |
| **SALES** | Sales Executive | Handles customer orders | Sales and customer features |
| **PRODUCTION** | Production Executive | Manages manufacturing | Production and quality features |
| **PURCHASE** | Purchase Executive | Buys materials and supplies | Purchase and inventory features |
| **STORE** | Store Manager | Manages inventory | Store and quality features |
| **CUSTOMER** | External Customer | Places and tracks orders | Customer portal features |

## Detailed Role Features

### 1. ADMIN Role
**Login**: Admin@MT

#### **What They Do**
- Oversee the entire business operations
- Monitor all departments and activities
- Make important business decisions

#### **What They Can Do**

##### **Business Overview**
- **View Complete Dashboard**: See all business performance at a glance
- **Track Sales Performance**: Monitor revenue and order statistics
- **Monitor Production**: Check manufacturing progress and efficiency
- **Review Financial Health**: See profits, expenses, and financial reports

##### **Sales Management**
- **View All Orders**: See every sales order in the system
- **Track Order Progress**: Monitor how orders are progressing
- **Manage Customers**: View and manage customer information
- **Generate Sales Reports**: Create reports on sales performance

##### **Production Management**
- **Plan Production**: Create and manage production schedules
- **Track Daily Production**: Monitor what's being manufactured each day
- **Check Production Status**: See which orders are in production
- **Generate Production Reports**: Create reports on manufacturing performance

##### **Inventory Management**
- **View All Inventory**: See complete stock levels across all warehouses
- **Manage Raw Materials**: Track steel, gears, and other materials
- **Manage Finished Products**: Oversee product catalog and stock
- **Monitor Stock Levels**: Get alerts when inventory is low

##### **Purchase Management**
- **Create Purchase Orders**: Order materials and supplies
- **Manage Suppliers**: Work with vendors and suppliers
- **Track Purchases**: Monitor what's being bought and when
- **Evaluate Suppliers**: Check supplier performance and quality

##### **Quality Control**
- **Handle Quality Issues**: Manage quality problems and complaints
- **Track Quality Problems**: Monitor quality issues across the business
- **Manage Supplier Quality**: Ensure suppliers meet quality standards
- **Generate Quality Reports**: Create reports on quality performance

##### **Financial Management**
- **Create Invoices**: Generate bills for customers
- **Process Payments**: Handle customer payments
- **Manage Finances**: Track all money coming in and going out
- **Generate Financial Reports**: Create profit and loss statements

##### **Delivery Management**
- **Track Deliveries**: Monitor shipments to customers
- **Generate Delivery Reports**: Create reports on delivery performance
- **Coordinate Logistics**: Manage shipping and delivery operations

## Business Process Overview

### How the System Works

#### **Sample Users in the System**

**Company Staff:**
- **Admin**: Manages everything in the business
- **Developer**: Sets up products and manages users
- **Sales**: Handles customer orders
- **Production**: Manages manufacturing
- **Purchase**: Buys materials and supplies
- **Store**: Manages inventory

**Customer Companies:**
- **GEAR WORLD**: Industrial gear manufacturer
- **KAPOOR SONS CORPORATION**: Engineering company
- **V S INDUSTRIES**: Industrial equipment supplier
- **KSR ENGINEERS**: Engineering solutions provider

### Product Categories

#### **Raw Materials**
- **Steel Components**: Base materials and castings
- **Mechanical Parts**: Gears, shafts, bearings
- **Hardware**: Fasteners, bolts, small components

#### **Finished Products**
- **Geared Motors**: Various power ratings and ratios
- **Helical Gearboxes**: Different configurations and specifications
- **Product Types**: Base (Foot), Vertical (Flange)

### Business Workflow

1. **Customer Places Order**: Customer orders products through the system
2. **Sales Processes Order**: Sales team reviews and approves the order
3. **Production Plans Manufacturing**: Production team creates manufacturing plan
4. **Purchase Orders Materials**: Purchase team buys required materials
5. **Production Manufactures**: Production team makes the products
6. **Quality Checks Products**: Quality team ensures products meet standards
7. **Order is Delivered**: Products are shipped to customer
8. **Invoice is Sent**: Customer receives bill and makes payment

### Key Benefits

- **Clear Role Separation**: Each person has specific responsibilities
- **Easy to Use**: Simple interface for each type of user
- **Complete Tracking**: Every order can be tracked from start to finish
- **Quality Control**: Built-in quality management system
- **Customer Portal**: Customers can track their own orders
- **Business Reports**: Generate reports for better decision making

The system supports the complete business process from customer orders to final delivery, with built-in quality control and reporting features to help the business grow and improve.
